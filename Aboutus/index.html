Who we are?

Inspired by the sea, tvoja drugarica is handmade elegant, sweet and effective jewerly designed for playful and romantic souls. Tvoja drugarica was born from an idea and need for reduced but conspicuous pieces.

Founded in 2021. We are constantly in touch with new trends and ideas. Tvoja drugarica creates handmade glass and perls jewelry exclusively for Serbian and Montenegrian market.She brings fashionable luxury to pieces designed to fit a contemporary, everyday lifestyle.

Our jewerly is made of stainless steel, glass pearls, freshwater pearls with natural sheels and 18k golden plate charms. Each piece is made carefully with quality materials and strong clasps.

In our offer you can find necklaces, bracelets, earrings and leg bracelets.
